Custom Made Lures

Our lures are custom made from old recycled baits, designed to attract a wide variety of fish species. Some designs are based on popular designs from top brands like Googan. The combination of baits creates many unique colors for the users. Not only do our lures help reduce waste by recycling old baits, but they also provide an unmatched fishing experience.
