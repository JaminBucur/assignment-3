Our Story

Founded in 2020 during the Coronavirus, our journey started between friends and family. We would go out and fish daily, catching all kinds of fish. We would catch buloogus that would leave us with ripped up and unusable baits. We felt it was wasteful to discard baits that were only used once. As a result, we came up with a way to reuse the baits by melting the leftovers down and remolding them into new fresh baits. This innovative approach not only reduces waste but also allows us to create unique and effective lures. Our recycled baits have since caught a wide variety of fish, proving that sustainability and performance can go hand in hand in the world of fishing.
